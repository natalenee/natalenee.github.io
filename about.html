About Me

Computer Engineering Student | Problem Solver | Creative Thinker

I'm Natalene Gunawan, a Computer Engineering student at the University of California, Irvine. I graduated from Mt. San Antonio College (Mt.SAC) in 2024 with an Associate's degree in Mathematics before transferring to UCI to pursue my passion for Computer Engineering.

My expertise comes with a strong foundation in both hardware and software along with a unique eye in artistic design. With a passion for drawing and design, I love being able to combine innovation with creative usability and functionality. This led me to pursue Computer Engineering in college where I could combine my love for both in a way that makes my work enjoyable and meaningful to use.

I love working at the intersection of hardware and software, building systems that solve real-world problems. When I'm not coding or designing circuits, you'll find me rock climbing, gaming, or diving into a good book! Check out some of my favorites below!
